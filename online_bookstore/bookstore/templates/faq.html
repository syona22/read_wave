Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I purchase a book?

You can browse our collection by genres or trending books and add them to your cart. Once you've selected the books you wish to purchase, simply proceed to checkout and make the payment using our secure payment gateway.

2. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, as well as PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

3. How do I access books after purchase?

After completing your purchase, you can access the books directly in your account under the "My Books" section. You can either read them online or download them for offline reading.

4. Can I return or exchange books?

Due to the nature of digital products, we do not accept returns or exchanges on purchased books. Please review the product details carefully before purchasing.

5. Do you offer physical copies of books?

Currently, we offer only digital versions of the books. Stay tuned for updates as we may introduce physical copies in the future.

6. How do I contact customer support?

If you have any issues or queries, you can reach our customer support via email at support@readwave.com or by calling us at 91 2318945679.
