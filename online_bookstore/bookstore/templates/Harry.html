Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

J.K. Rowling

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone is the first book in the renowned Harry Potter series written by J.K. Rowling. The story begins with Harry Potter, an orphaned boy who lives with his neglectful relatives, the Dursleys. On his eleventh birthday, Harry learns that he is a wizard and is invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. At Hogwarts, Harry befriends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, and together they uncover a mystery surrounding the theft of the Sorcerer's Stone, an object that grants immortality. They suspect that the dark wizard Lord Voldemort is plotting to find the stone and return to power..
